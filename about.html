About Me

Hi! My name is Amie. I am a clinical research manager at a small start-up focused on womens health. After working in the same field for so long I wanted to try something completley different. I came across SheCodes while scrolling through my Instagram and decided to check it out. I took the free introduction to coding class first and was instantly intrigued and wanted to learn more. I initially only signed up for the basic SheCodes workshop but had such a great experience I decided to sign up for the pro membership to complete all the offered workshops. I never thought I would be able to learn to code let along be able to build my own website. I really have enjoyed my coding journey and cannot wait to continue coding! Keep and eye on what I am working on next.
